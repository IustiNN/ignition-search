Solutions

In order to follow consumer paths and search habits, we kicked things off with a singular shopping campaign comprised of their most profitable items. Once this started to populate and increase profitability, we used the data to hone the campaign down into specific categories aligned with transactional shopping vs. window shopping and browsing. As a result, we’ve been able to focus on best performing product areas and maximise ROI.

To cement Kitlocker’s spot amongst the big online players, we undertook original research to discover how we could further improve consumer experience in such a mature, well - established market. The results were used to create useful, compelling articles which were picked up by numerous mainstream media outlets in the football industry.
