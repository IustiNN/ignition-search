Shortlisted at the Northern eCommerce Awards 2017!

News & Events 4/09/2017

Scroll for more

We’re proud to announce that we have been shortlisted for the Best eCommerce search/marketing campaign as part of the Northern eCommerce Awards 2017. Open to companies across the north of England, their aim is to recognise the region as a hub for eCommerce growth and innovation.

The judges were looking for clear objectives with demonstrable results and we’re delighted that our work with Pretty Green has been recognised as it represents the perfect case study on how best practices, fine tuning details and granular control on a very simple level can maximise results and exceed client’s expectations.

Answering to millions of search queries, shopping ads have significantly improved the way consumers shop, making Google Shopping an increasingly important channel for driving eCommerce revenue. Our ability to greatly improve and enhance the quality of the product feed, were key success factors in achieving a revenue of over £1 million in June alone from a £45k budget, proving the efficiency of this channel when everything is done right.

The awards will take place on 11 October during the eCommerce Show North and will be the North’s biggest celebration of success and talent across e-retailing and online business. We’ll keep you posted!

With our experience and proven track record, we are the ideal partner for anyone looking to take their business forward. For an informal chat about your digital strategy and how we can help, contact us online or call today on 0114 272 2444.
