Solutions

Targeting areas around showrooms, we created multiple local campaigns based on user searches and refined them over time to generate sample requests, store searches, call-back requests and live chats. Following UX best practices we introduced ecommerce optimisation focusing on people, not products, to drive efficiencies and grow brand loyalties

To increase footfall and monitor in-store visits, we implemented a new Beta feature by Google allowing us to track in-store visits from PPC activity and optimise campaigns in line with online actions and physical store visits.
